Module Submissions

Below is a quick overview of the general review process. Authors are able to submit:

  • Module Idea: authors will be paired with a coach to help develop the module which can then be submitted to SCORE for a full review
  • (Nearly) Completed Module: authors submit their module files as a zipped folder to begin the full review process
